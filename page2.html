Project Descriptions

Meditation Artifacts MVP

Leading the design and user experience for a groundbreaking meditation app developed by Harvard religious studies scholars, I created a minimal viable product (MVP) using Figma to produce interactive wireframes. These wireframes received positive feedback on the UI appeal and brand differentiation from investors, stakeholders and advisors, including former CTO of Kayak (Paul English) and are pivotal in securing further investment. The wireframes were also tested among potential users (those who would pay for the product and/or signed up for future beta testing) through survey testing and expressed positive emotions towards the design and feel of the product. Key Achievements: Developed MVP for investor presentation. Established design principles, color and general style guidelines for the MAI brand. Skills: User Interface (UI), Product Lifecycle, User Experience (UX), Figma

AgentAI Web App

Designed and developed the user interface for an AI-driven IT chat tool at Harvard Kennedy School with another frontend designer and developer. This tool, developed in compliance with Harvard's design guidelines, will reduce IT support requests by approximately 50% through efficient, tailored responses and an accessible, user-friendly UI. Key Achievements: Cut assistance needs by ~50% through enhanced UI/UX design. Languages: JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, HTML Skills: Design with Generative AI, Product Lifecycle, AI Ethical Policy, Accessibility, Security, Figma
